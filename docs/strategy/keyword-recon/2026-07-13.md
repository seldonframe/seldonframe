## 0. Method notes / deviations from spec

- This DataForSEO account only accepts **one task per request** on `serp/google/organic/live/regular` (batch of 15 tasks in one POST returned `40000 You can set only one task at a time` for 14 of 15). Re-ran as 15 separate single-task requests.
- `depth=100` on that endpoint costs **10x** the base rate ($0.016/query, not ~$0.003). To stay near budget, all 15 SERP checks ran at **depth=10** instead of depth=100 — good enough for page-shape classification (needs top 5) and directional rank-tracking, but a `seldonframe.com` result ranking at position 11–100 would not be caught this run. Worth remembering for next week's run: budget ~$0.02/keyword if depth=100 is required.
- 126 keywords fit in one bulk `search_volume` call ($0.09) — nowhere near the 700/request ceiling.
- Total spend landed at $0.172 vs the ~$0.15 target, because the gap-mining Labs call ($0.036) was run in full per spec rather than skipped. Flagging honestly rather than silently overshooting next time — if the cap is meant to be hard, cut the SERP sample from 15→10 keywords next run to bank the headroom.

## 3. THE DELIVERABLE — next 10 pages to build (ranked by volume × fit × winnability)

## 5. Shape-mismatch flags (2 confirmed — both from the 15 SERP-checked keywords)

**FIX 1 — "gohighlevel alternative" (390/mo).** Top 5: 4 of 5 are multi-option listicles (whatconverts "7 GoHighLevel Alternatives," simular.ai "9 Better Options," heyflow "6 GoHighLevel alternatives," perspective.co "7 Best Tools Compared") + 1 Reddit thread. Our `/alternative-to-gohighlevel` page is a single one-vs-one comparison (SF vs GHL only) — the wrong shape for what Google ranks here. **Re-shape:** add a "Top GoHighLevel alternatives in 2026" listicle section/page (SF positioned #1, same honest multi-contender format already used in `best-pages.ts`), rather than relying on the pure head-to-head page to rank for this exact query.

**FIX 2 — "klaviyo alternative" (390/mo).** Same pattern: 4 of 5 top results are listicles (maestra.io "12 Best Klaviyo Alternatives," campaignmonitor "16 Klaviyo Alternatives," emailvendorselection "12 Best," even klaviyo.com's own comparison page) + 1 Reddit thread. Our `/alternative-to-klaviyo` page is the same single-comparison shape. **Re-shape:** same fix as FIX 1 — a listicle framing, not just the 1:1 comparison.

All other 13 SERP-checked keywords showed our existing page shape already matches (or reasonably approximates) what Google ranks — pricing pages against pricing pages, the `best-website-builder-for-small-businesses` listicle against other listicles.
