# Founder shot list + VO script (v2 launch video, ~75s)

Two filmed clips (A-roll) + one VO track. Film both clips in one sitting — under 30 minutes
total. The video is built with timed placeholder slots; send me the files and I drop them in
and re-sync every beat to your voice (word-level timestamps).

## Setup (once)

- **Location:** clean wall with depth behind you (plants/brick/shelf — not a flat white wall).
  Daylight from a window at 45°, or one lamp bounced off the wall behind the camera.
- **Camera:** phone, horizontal, 4K if available, lens at eye level, arm's length + a step.
- **Audio:** record VO separately on the phone's voice-memo app held ~20cm from your mouth,
  in a closet or car if the room echoes. The camera mic is backup only.
- **Wardrobe:** plain dark top (forest/black). No logos.
- Do 3+ takes of everything; keep rolling between takes.

Look straight into the lens. Energy up, pace unhurried. One line:

> "If you sell websites, AI agents, or automations to local businesses, you know the drill —
> weeks of setup per client, meters on every text, and a platform you can never leave.
> We built the opposite."

(The opening callout names the actual ICP — GHL resellers, web shops, AI-agent builders —
instead of "every agency". Alternates if this feels stiff on camera: the reseller dagger

is what it should have been." — narrower but maximum debate-bait.)

(The category line "This is SeldonFrame — the AI front office platform agencies actually
own" lands as on-screen text right after your clip, so end on "the opposite" with a beat of
held eye contact.)
