# BLOCK: Client Intake & Onboarding Portal

**Description**  
Create a client-facing intake and onboarding portal that captures goals, assets, kickoff details, and next steps in one guided flow.

**Trigger Phrases**  
- "Set up a client intake portal for new leads"
- "Create onboarding for new coaching or agency clients"

**Behavior**  
Guide a new client from intake form to kickoff readiness with clear status tracking. Capture goals, assets, timeline, budget, approvals, and preferred communication style, then convert submissions into CRM records, tasks, and onboarding milestones. Surface actionable patterns such as delayed approvals, missing documents, and stalled kickoff steps so the team can shorten time-to-value and improve client conversion into active delivery.

**Integration Points**  
- CRM
- Brain v2
- Forms
- Booking
- Email
- Pages
