# Waitlist Form

## Purpose
Capture launch interest and qualified leads before full product release.

## Default Behavior
- Add a simple waitlist form on the primary landing flow.
- Collect name, email, company, and optional use-case details.
- Store submissions in CRM contacts and tag with `waitlist`.

## Notes
Use for product-led launches and pre-release validation.
