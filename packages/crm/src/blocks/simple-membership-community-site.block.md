# BLOCK: Simple Membership & Community Site

**Description**  
Create a lightweight membership and community experience for paid subscribers or early supporters.

**Trigger Phrases**  
- "Create a simple paid membership site"
- "Set up a community area for members"

**Behavior**  
Provide an easy way to gate content, member updates, onboarding resources, and community prompts behind a paid or invite-only experience. Track which content types drive retention, upgrades, and re-engagement rather than simply counting logins. Favor actionable business patterns such as which onboarding sequence keeps members active through week two and which community prompts correlate with renewals.

**Integration Points**  
- CRM
- Payments
- Brain v2
- Email
- Pages
- Portal
