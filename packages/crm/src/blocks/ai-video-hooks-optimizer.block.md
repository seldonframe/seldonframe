# BLOCK: AI Video Generation Hooks Optimizer

**Description**  
Create a workflow that tests, stores, and improves high-converting video hooks for ecommerce product campaigns.

**Trigger Phrases**  
- "Optimize hooks for my ecommerce videos"
- "Find better video openers for product ads"

**Behavior**  
Capture video concepts, first-three-second hooks, creative angles, product context, audience segment, and performance results in a repeatable optimization loop. Compare hooks by conversion rate, watch-through, click-through, and add-to-cart impact so the system can recommend which opening patterns deserve reuse. Favor concrete business insights such as urgency hooks outperforming feature-first intros for beauty launches, or testimonial-led hooks lifting add-to-cart rate for higher-ticket bundles.

**Integration Points**  
- CRM
- Brain v2
- Analytics
- Pages
- Forms
