# BLOCK: Bulk Video Generation & Performance Tracker

**Description**  
Create a system for generating product video batches, tracking performance, and identifying the best-performing creative patterns at scale.

**Trigger Phrases**  
- "Generate and track bulk product videos"
- "Create a dashboard for batch video performance"

**Behavior**  
Manage bulk video production runs across collections, SKUs, offers, and campaign angles while tying each asset to downstream performance metrics. Track which combinations of hook, CTA, duration, format, and product category produce the strongest revenue, ROAS, and watch-time outcomes so operators can scale winners and cut weak variants quickly. Emphasize actionable patterns such as 12-second urgency videos lifting conversion for seasonal collections or creator-style demo videos outperforming static showcases for bundles.

**Integration Points**  
- CRM
- Brain v2
- Analytics
- Pages
- Payments
