# BLOCK: Client Dashboard & Progress Analytics

**Description**  
Create a client dashboard that shows delivery progress, outcomes, blockers, and next recommended actions.

**Trigger Phrases**  
- "Build a client dashboard with progress analytics"
- "Show clients milestones, wins, and blockers in one place"

**Behavior**  
Display milestones, tasks, booked sessions, deliverable status, KPI snapshots, and upcoming actions in a single dashboard. Highlight risk patterns such as slipping timelines, low engagement, or stalled approvals, and pair them with next-step recommendations for both the client and the service team. Focus the experience on measurable outcomes so progress is tied to retention, upsell readiness, and customer confidence rather than generic reporting.

**Integration Points**  
- CRM
- Brain v2
- Analytics
- Booking
- Email
- Pages
