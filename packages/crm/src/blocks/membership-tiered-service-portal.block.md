# BLOCK: Membership & Tiered Service Portal

**Description**  
Create a client portal for tiered service delivery with gated resources, billing visibility, and upgrade paths.

**Trigger Phrases**  
- "Create a tiered client membership portal"
- "Set up Bronze Silver Gold service access"

**Behavior**  
Provide a secure portal where clients can view plan-specific resources, deliverables, session credits, invoices, and renewal details. Support tier upgrades, downgrade requests, and usage visibility so teams can spot expansion opportunities and churn risk early. Emphasize business patterns like which tier combinations retain longest, which offers drive upgrades, and where delivery bottlenecks reduce perceived value.

**Integration Points**  
- CRM
- Payments
- Brain v2
- Email
- Pages
- Portal
