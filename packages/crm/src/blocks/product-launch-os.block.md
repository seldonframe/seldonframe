# BLOCK: Product Launch OS

**Description**  
Create a launch operating system that coordinates waitlist capture, launch assets, conversion tracking, and post-launch follow-up.

**Trigger Phrases**  
- "Set up an operating system for my product launch"
- "Create a launch workflow for my SaaS or micro-SaaS"

**Behavior**  
Coordinate launch planning across landing pages, waitlist or checkout flows, campaign assets, launch calendar, and follow-up sequences. Track performance by channel, offer, launch phase, and message angle so founders can see what actually drives signups, sales, and activation. Emphasize concrete patterns such as which assets convert best during prelaunch, which launch-day motions create the largest revenue spikes, and which follow-ups improve activation after purchase.

**Integration Points**  
- CRM
- Brain v2
- Payments
- Email
- Pages
- Analytics
