# viktor-light

**Vibe:** white background, editorial restraint, Instrument Serif italic
accents on emphasized words. No background video. Narrow centered
column with staggered fade-rise entrance. Reads like a founder's
personal page or a thoughtful agency's pitch.

**When to use:** light editorial brands — solo coaches, indie founders,
freelance creatives, boutique agencies, founder-led service businesses.
The default for `technical-restrained` and `editorial-warm` archetypes
when the workspace isn't dashboard-product-shaped.

**Copy pattern:** Short, declarative headlines (4-8 words). The
`shinyWord` becomes serif italic in a muted color — pick a noun or
verb that carries the brand's stance. Subheadline reads like a
personal sentence, not marketing copy.

**Headline examples (good):**
- "Build the next wave, the bold way." + shinyWord "bold"
- "Design that earns the second meeting." + shinyWord "earns"
- "Brands deserve more than a logo refresh." + shinyWord "deserve"

**Consumes:** kicker, headline, shinyWord, subheadline, ctaText,
ctaLink, secondaryCta, proofTile, riskReversalBadges. Does NOT use
heroVideo or heroImage — purely typographic.
