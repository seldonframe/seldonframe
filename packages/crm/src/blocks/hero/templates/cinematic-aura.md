# cinematic-aura

**Vibe:** dark, sensory, premium. Looping Pexels MP4 + liquid-glass UI +
Instrument Serif italic + Framer Motion blur-in headline. One emphatic
word in the headline renders with a cyan-gradient shimmer.

**When to use:** lifestyle businesses where customers buy the dream
before the service — luxe coaching, medspa, wellness, fitness, premium
salons, holistic services. Anywhere the photo of the work would be
secondary to the *feeling* of the work.

**Copy pattern:** 2-line headlines work best. Pick a `shinyWord` that's
an outcome noun — what the customer walks away with. Avoid numerals
in the shiny word (Instrument Serif italic looks awkward on digits;
put numbers in the kicker or subhead instead).

**Headline examples (good):**
- "Look Like Yourself, Only Quieter" + shinyWord "Quieter"
- "The Strength You Forgot You Had" + shinyWord "Strength"
- "Skin That Stops the Conversation" + shinyWord "Conversation"

**Consumes:** kicker, headline, shinyWord, subheadline, ctaText,
ctaLink, secondaryCta, proofTile, riskReversalBadges, heroVideo,
heroVideoAttribution.
