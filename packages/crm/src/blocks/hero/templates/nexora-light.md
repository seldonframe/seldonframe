# nexora-light

**Vibe:** white background, B2B SaaS confidence, Instrument Serif italic
on one emphasized word. A custom-coded CRM + booking-calendar mockup
sits below the hero copy — the same shape every SeldonFrame workspace
actually ships, so the mockup doubles as a product preview ("this is
what you actually get"). No background video.

**When to use:** B2B SaaS founders, productivity tools, agencies that
position as "we run your back-office", AI tools that have a clear
in-app UI to showcase. Anywhere the operator's customers will see a
dashboard.

**Copy pattern:** "The Future of [X] [Y]" / "Stop [verbing] and start
[other-verbing]" / "[Outcome] in [timeframe]". One italic word ideally
acts as the differentiator (Smarter, Faster, Honest, Quiet).

**Headline examples (good):**
- "The Future of Smarter Automation" + shinyWord "Smarter"
- "Run a Quieter Inbox" + shinyWord "Quieter"
- "Pipeline that Closes Itself" + shinyWord "Closes"

**Consumes:** kicker, headline, shinyWord, subheadline, ctaText,
ctaLink, secondaryCta. Renders the embedded CRM/booking dashboard
mockup statically — no other props needed for that.
