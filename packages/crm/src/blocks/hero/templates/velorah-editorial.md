# velorah-editorial

**Vibe:** dark editorial luxe. Pexels MP4 background under a deep-navy
gradient wash. Instrument Serif italic emphasis on the `shinyWord`
(softer than cinematic-aura's gradient shine — just serif treatment in
a muted color for contrast). Generous vertical breathing room, centered
text, restrained motion.

**When to use:** luxe service businesses that prefer editorial mood
over SaaS-shiny — premium coaches, creative studios, design-led
agencies, high-end consultants. When the customer is buying taste +
discernment + judgement, not features.

**Copy pattern:** "Where X Y the Z" structures work beautifully here.
Two-clause headlines with a single italicized noun. Subheadline carries
the *who-for* explicitly.

**Headline examples (good):**
- "Where dreams rise through the silence." + shinyWord "silence"
- "Strategy that earns the front page." + shinyWord "earns"
- "Brand systems built once, used for decades." + shinyWord "decades"

**Consumes:** kicker, headline, shinyWord, subheadline, ctaText,
ctaLink, secondaryCta, heroVideo, heroVideoAttribution. Skips proofTile
+ riskReversalBadges (editorial restraint — those clutter the look).
